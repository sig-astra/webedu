Sig Astra

I am an IT professional and technology enthusiast. If it runs on electricity, you can bet I’ve dabbled in it. I’m what you’d call a tech-of-all-trades. In terms of my area of expertise, I work mainly with Linux systems, but I always have Windows ready for some special tools. I work a lot with operating systems; typically, I enjoy installing and configuring software more than actually using it. That's where a lot of my experience comes from: build it, fix it, break it, fix it, repeat.

I study cybersecurity and networking at Year Up Chicago. The great thing about cyber is that you really need a holistic background in IT; when securing a system, you need to know the potential vulnerabilities at every level from open network ports to clipboard loggers. In the future, I plan to obtain the Red Hat RHCA, the Cisco CCAr, and the Microsoft MCA, which will be integral in my long-term career.
