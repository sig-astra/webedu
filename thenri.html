T'Henri Caldwell

T’henri Caldwell, was born and raised in Chicago Illinois who is the eldest son and third oldest child in his family. Since the age of 9, he has since then had a fascination with computer technology due to observing an IT specialist perform of a computer system at the time. Over the course of time, T’henri has been undertaking efforts to learning about the machinations of computer science with high and low points; but he was able to preserver through many hardships that would have otherwise demoralized him.

In the winter of 2013, Mr. Caldwell had enrolled into the Sanford-Brown College of Chicago (formerly known as IADT) and graduated with an Associate’s degree in Computer Networking. T’henri is currently enrolled in the Year Up Chicago program to further his knowledge in IT, as well as building a professional brand for himself to be incorporated into the corporate industry. T’henri is currently at the endpoint of the Year Up L&D process and is set to enter an internship position that was his initial role since the beginning of the program.

In the past 6 years, T’henri has been striving to educate himself in various skills and professions of interests. Although computer science is his primary current focus, he has always been seeking new goals and achievements whenever the opportunity arises. Throughout the majority of his years of youth, T’henri has been known to most of his associates and family as a quiet individual a shy individual who tends to himself. However, he has grown to develop a sense of observation and has been noted by many associated to him as being a heavy thinker when it comes to issues for discussions.
